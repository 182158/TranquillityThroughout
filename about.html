About The Team



At Tranquillity Throughout, our mission is to create a safe space where users can share advice, relaxation techniques and experiences. Users can use the advice and experiences to create their own support system. We want to create a community to support our users but also give them the tools to build themselves up: ensuring themselves they don't have to rely on others for support.



Morgan Wills - Project Lead and Instagram Manager

Morgan is the project leader for Tranquillity Throughout and spearheaded the concept. Leading the small team, he tries to support his team while dedicating himself to Tranquillity Throughout. As our in house person with mental health issues, Morgan used his Anxiety and Depression to bring this project to life.
