Advice and Experiences


Adivce and Experience from other users

At Tranquillity Throughout, we want to offer something that no other service that focuses on help does: a community. We want to give others the opportunity to build up a network and establish a community, where they can share experiences or advice in order to possibly help others. We want our communityto blossom and grow in order to help build up support for each other but our end goal is to have the user be able to become fully self reliant.







Adivce

Marcus Wilkins

When dealing with my depression, I often found that counting to 10 when imagining something can help me ease away from depressive episodes. For example, I count to 10 while imagining water drops falling from a leaf after a storm. It really calms me down and helps me focus.


Tamara Lee

Take up some form of exercise. There's evidence that exercise can help lift your mood. If you haven't exercised for a while, start gently by walking for 20 minutes every day.
